Information

This is the information page for this experimental dashboard. This dashboard is part of a platform where coaches can monitor and keep updated regarding their clients. The information page is up and running as well as the follow up page.This dashboard is from the clients point of view. The follow up page is where the client will weekly update their coach about their improvements. The "plans" page is where the client will be provided with downaodable files or links where they can access their workout and meal plans, this part will not be updated in MS1. Progress page is where the cleint will be able to review their information and progress, this part will be enabled after MS1.
